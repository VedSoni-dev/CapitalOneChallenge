Welcome to FinancingForFood

FinancingForFood is an interactive financial decision-making game designed to test and improve your financial literacy. Navigate through real-life financial scenarios, make critical choices, and learn how to manage your money effectively.

Game Overview

  • Start with a random initial balance between $100 and $2000
  • Face 15 unique financial scenarios
  • Make choices that impact your financial health
  • Goal: Increase your balance and demonstrate financial wisdom

Winning Conditions

  • Finish with a balance higher than your starting amount
  • Achieve a performance score of 75% or higher
  • Survive all 15 scenarios without going broke

How to Play

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin your financial journey
  2. Read each scenario carefully
  3. Choose the best financial option
  4. Watch your balance and track your progress
  5. Learn from each decision and improve your financial skills
